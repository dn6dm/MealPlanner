About this website

The MealPlanner website is for anyone looking to regulate their caloric and macronutrient intake more thoroughly. From Weight Watchers to the Keto diet, there are an endless number of different diets and meal plans. These diets all differ in principles and approaches but the most important thing is whether or not you can stick through with it. Most people often fail to follow through with their diets because it is way too different from what they usually eat or cook and takes too much effort to maintain. The MealPlanner website seeks to create healthier diets that users can actually keep up with and maintain long-term.

How it works

The MealPlanner works by taking a macro and calorie focused approach. You'll first be asked to input your macronutrient goals in grams of protein, carbohydrates, and fats. Afterwards, you can choose to add the nutritional information of the foods that you would like to use in your diet. The planner also comes with some nutritional information already pre-added to its database to help meet the macro needs of your meal plan. Once you've added your foods, the planner will devise a meal plan that suits your macro needs. If you decide that you like the meal plan and want to save it for later, you can choose to favorite it. If not, you can get the planner to make another meal plan for you.

Things to note

Please note that if you choose to add many foods that are poorly proportioned macro-wise, the calculator will draw more upon the pre-added foods in the database to construct your meal plan as to meet your needs. Furthermore, while the planner does draw upon the principles of the If It Fits Your Macros diet, it is suggested that you focus primarily on eating healthy foods with the occasional indulgence as that will help your body regulate hunger better.
